Documentation for the California Monthly plugin

About the Plugin

The California Monthly plugin provides automatic monthly adjustment of irrigation times based on historical weather data. It is intended as an aid for water conservation.

This plugin adjusts the run times for all scheduled irrigation programs by a percentage which is calculated from the Monthly Average Reference Evapotranspiration by ETo Zone table found on page 3 of the linked PDF document.

http://wwwcimis.water.ca.gov/Content/pdf/CimisRefEvapZones.pdf

Reference ETo is an estimate of the amount of water removed from the soil by evaporation from the soil surface and by plants (transpiration) in units of inches using a standard type of plant. You can think of the ETo value as the amount of rain (in inches) it would take to replace the water that has been removed.

The calculation divides the average ETo for each month by the maximum ETo value for the selected zone.

ETo / Maximum ETo * 100 (rounded to a whole percentage)

Using the California Monthly plugin

  1. Click the "FIND YOUR ETO ZONE" button. A new tab will open in your browser with the REFRENCE EVAPOTRANSPIRATION ZONES document loaded.
  2. Scroll down to the zone map on page 2 and find the zone for your location.
  3. Enter your zone number in the "ETo Zone" box.
  4. Click "CALCULATE". The monthly adjustments will appear as percentages of scheduled watering times.
    You may optionally change the percentages manually but it is recommended to keep the calculated values and make changes to your irrigation schedules to suit your local conditions.
  5. Click "SUBMIT" at the bottom of the page to save your settings and return to the home page.

Note:
The month with the highest ETo value will appear with an adjustment of 100% This is the month with the highest water use on average. Adjusting your irrigation schedules during this month to provide adequate water to your plants should give you a good base line for the rest of the year. However, it is always wise to remain vigilant for changes in conditions.
